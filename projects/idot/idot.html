background:

Innovative Designers of Tomorrow (IDOT) is a
non-profit organization started by UC Berkeley
students that aims to bring accessible,
comprehensive, and empathetic design
education to high school students in a
student-driven, club-based format.

role:

I was a Graphic Designer on the Marketing
team for the founding semester of IDOT,
where I created various illustrations and
promotional materials, usually in Figma,
for our social media and club events.
Every graphic was the result of an extremely
iterative process - collaboration was key,
as my team held weekly meetings to
brainstorm content ideas, provide feedback,
and iterate over designs together.
